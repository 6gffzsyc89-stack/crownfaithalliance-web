We build the software we run on

A great back office means moving information fast and getting the busywork out of the way. When the tools we needed didn't exist, we built them — consumer-friendly software you just open and use. The same tools that run our own properties are the products we now offer.

Yomied · the back-office brain

Your back office, in the chats you already use

An assistant that lives inside WhatsApp, WeChat and Telegram. Add it to a chat and it quietly organises everything it learns, then answers your questions right there — in your language, with the proof attached. No app to learn, no files to manage.

  • Reconciles every payment to its receipt and bank line
  • Aggregates guest reviews and drafts replies for approval
